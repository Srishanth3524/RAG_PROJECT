Cricket is one of the most popular sports in the world. It is especially famous in countries like India, Australia, England, and Pakistan. The game is played between two teams of eleven players each.

The objective of cricket is to score more runs than the opposing team. A match is played on a circular or oval-shaped ground with a rectangular pitch in the center. The pitch is 22 yards long and has wickets at both ends.

There are three main formats of cricket: Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20). Test matches are played over five days, while ODIs and T20s are limited-overs formats.

Batting, bowling, and fielding are the three essential skills in cricket. Batsmen try to score runs, bowlers try to dismiss batsmen, and fielders attempt to prevent runs and take catches.

The International Cricket Council (ICC) governs international cricket and organizes major tournaments like the Cricket World Cup and the T20 World Cup.
